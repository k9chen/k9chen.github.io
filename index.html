Kelvin Chen; or, Kaiwen

I am a third-year undergraduate at UVA majoring in neuroscience and chemistry with a minor in bioethics. I am respectively advised by Drs. Petr Tvrdik and Richard Dortch in the Departments of Neurosurgery and Translational Neuroscience at UVA School of Medicine and Barrow Neurological Institute, where I conduct CNS/PNS disease-oriented preclinical research.

My main research focuses on translating pathophysiological biomarkers and mechanisms of neurovascular diseases into novel diagnostic and therapeutic tools for clinical applications via the integration of computational and experimental methods.
